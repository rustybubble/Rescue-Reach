Foundation

Rescue Reach was founded in 2023 with the mission to enhance beach safety through innovative technology. Our team is dedicated to developing solutions that help lifeguards and beachgoers stay safe from hidden dangers in the water.

Purpose

Our purpose is to provide lifeguards with advanced tools to detect and respond to rip currents and other hazards. By leveraging AI and drone technology, we aim to make beaches safer for everyone.

The Problem

Rip currents are a significant hazard at beaches worldwide, causing numerous drownings and rescues each year. Traditional methods of detecting rip currents are often insufficient, leaving beachgoers at risk.

Our Impact

Since our inception, Rescue Reach has made a substantial impact on beach safety. Our AI-powered drones have successfully detected and alerted lifeguards to numerous rip currents, preventing potential drownings and saving lives. We continue to innovate and improve our technology to ensure the highest level of safety for beachgoers.
